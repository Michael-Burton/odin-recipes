Lasagna





Description

This is a custom-made lasagna made entirely of FOSS. It may not look appetizing, but I promise you that it is delicious.


Ingredients

  • 16 oz lasagna noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 32 oz ricotta cheese
  • Chives, if you like
  • Pasta sauce
  • 16 oz mozzerella cheese, shredded
  • 4 oz parmesean cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Boil noodles in a pot of water
  3. Meanwhile, mix ricotta, chives, and salt and pepper to taste
  4. Drain and rinse noodles in a strainer
  5. Grease a lasagna pan (13x9 will do)
  6. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce on the bottom of the pan
  7. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles
  8. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture
  9. Add 4 oz mozzerella, 1 oz parmesean
  10. Add a layer of pasta sauce on top
  11. Repeat steps 7-10 2 more times
  12. Top last layer of pasta with pasta sauce, then the remaining cheese
  13. Cover with aluminum foil, and bake for 30 minutes
  14. Uncover, and bake for 25-30 more
  15. Remove from oven, let cool for a few minutes, and enjoy!
